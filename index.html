Hello! Welcome!!

I am a Research Scientist at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP), University of Colorado Boulder.

My research focuses on the upper atmospheres, ionospheres, and exospheres of planets, with a particular interest in Mars and Venus, their atmospheric escape, and evolution. I am also greatly interested in space weather and heliophysics.

In my leisure time, I enjoy outdoors, traveling, tennis, and biking. My pronouns are he/him/his.

✉️ krishnaprasad.chirakkil@lasp.colorado.edu
